# Sources & provenance

All customer verbatims and category figures in this corpus are SYNTHETIC — no
real customer data or PII. The complaint-category taxonomy and benchmark
percentages are directional, grounded in public regulator disclosures:

- Commission for Complaints for Telecom-television Services (CCTS, Canada),
  2024-25 annual report: billing reported as the leading issue (~46%), service
  delivery ~25%.
- US FCC consumer-complaint disclosures (as summarised in public CX statistics):
  the large majority of telecom complaints concern service quality, billing, or
  provisioning.
- Ofcom (UK) telecoms & pay-TV complaints publications: recurring categories
  include complaint handling, faults/service/provisioning, and switching.

These are used as directional context only. Live, current benchmarks are pulled
at runtime by the web-search agent.
