# Category: Network & Coverage (synthetic verbatims)

Definition: complaints about call quality, dropped calls, no/low signal, and poor
indoor coverage. Often spikes around specific cells or after network changes.

Representative (synthetic) verbatims:
- "Calls drop every evening near the city-centre tower since last month."
- "No signal inside my office building though outdoor coverage is fine."
- "4G shows full bars but calls keep failing during peak hours."

Operational note: network/faults/provisioning is a recurring top category in
regulator data; pair with cell-site performance and recent change tickets when
investigating a spike.
