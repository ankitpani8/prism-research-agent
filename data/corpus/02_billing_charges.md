# Category: Billing & Charges (synthetic verbatims)

Definition: incorrect charges, unexpected balance deductions, missing promised
credits, and disputed premium-service charges. Historically the largest single
driver of telecom complaints.

Representative (synthetic) verbatims:
- "I was charged for a data pack I never activated."
- "My main balance dropped by 30 units overnight with no usage."
- "Promised cashback credit from the recharge offer never arrived."

Operational note: billing accounted for ~46% of issues in one national ombudsman's
annual report; incorrect charges and missing credits are the usual sub-drivers.
