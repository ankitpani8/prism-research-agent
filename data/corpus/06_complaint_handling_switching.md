# Category: Complaint Handling & Switching (synthetic verbatims)

Definition: dissatisfaction with how complaints are handled (slow resolution,
repeated contacts, no callback) and difficulty switching/porting out.

Representative (synthetic) verbatims:
- "Third call about the same billing issue and still no resolution."
- "Was promised a callback in 24 hours; it's been a week."
- "Trying to port out but keep getting rejection SMS with no reason."

Operational note: complaint handling itself is a leading source of dissatisfaction
in regulator data, and switching is frequently the second-ranked driver.
