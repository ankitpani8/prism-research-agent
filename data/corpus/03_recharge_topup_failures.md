# Category: Recharge & Top-up Failures (synthetic verbatims)

Definition: payment debited but prepaid balance not credited, failed UPI/card
top-ups, and delayed credit after recharge.

Representative (synthetic) verbatims:
- "UPI payment succeeded but my recharge didn't reflect for two hours."
- "Card was charged twice but balance updated once."
- "Recharge failed on the app yet money was deducted from my account."

Operational note: recharge-failure complaints cluster around payment-gateway
incidents; correlate with gateway error rates and reconciliation lag.
